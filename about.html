The origin story

Why we built
Rocket Routines

"The businesses that need automation the most are exactly the ones who can't afford a dev team — and can't get enterprise software to fit their specific process."

Our founder Colin Edwards spent over 20 years working across automotive e-commerce, supply chain, and operations — building ERP integrations, supplier data pipelines, e-commerce platforms, and the automation layer that ties them together. That work was done for employers who had the budget for a full technical team.

What kept showing up were the businesses just below that level — 30 to 200 employees, doing $3M to $30M in revenue — drowning in exactly the same manual work. Real operational complexity, real costs from inefficiency, and no one to fix it affordably. Enterprise software was too expensive and too rigid. Freelancers built one thing and left. Agencies pitched decks and delivered slowly.

Rocket Routines was built to fill that gap. Not just automating individual workflows — but designing the end-to-end growth system: from how leads come in, to how they're processed, to how the business operates once they become clients. Our team handles everything — strategy, build, and ongoing maintenance — so nothing falls through the cracks.

The Pilot tier exists because we know what it's like to ask a business to trust a new partner with their operations. Start small, prove it works, then build the system. That's the commitment we make to every client from day one.

How we work

What you can expect
working with us

01
We diagnose before we build
The free audit isn't a sales call — it's a genuine diagnostic. We map your current systems, find the real bottlenecks, and tell you honestly what automation would and wouldn't fix. Sometimes the answer is simpler than you think.
02
We own the outcome, not just the output
Most contractors build something and hand it over. We stay on retainer because the systems need to be maintained, monitored, and expanded as your business changes. Your growth is the measure of success — not the number of workflows built.
03
We operate with minimal friction
One kickoff call. Milestone updates. Monthly reports. You're not our project manager — we figure things out independently and bring you the outcome, not the process. Your time is too valuable to spend on status meetings.
04
We price honestly
Fixed setup fees. Flat monthly retainers. No hourly billing, no "scope creep" surprises. What we quote is what you pay. The Pilot tier exists because we'd rather earn your trust with a small win than ask for a big commitment upfront.
05
You own everything we build
Every workflow, credential, and document is yours. If we ever stop working together, you leave with a fully documented system and a handover session. We build things that belong to you — not things that keep you dependent on us.
06
We can connect any platform
Twenty years of reading API documentation means our team can pick up almost any platform quickly. If your tool isn't on our list, tell us what it does and we'll figure out how to connect it. Niche software is usually where the best automation opportunities hide.
