How the funnel works

From first click to closed sale —
automated end to end

Every stage is connected. When a lead moves, the system moves with them — no manual hand-offs, no dropped balls.

01
Traffic & Ad Capture
Paid ads, organic search, or social — all routed to a purpose-built landing page optimized for one action only.
02
Lead Capture & Qualify
A ClickFunnels opt-in page collects the right info and instantly tags, scores, and segments the lead in your CRM.
03
Nurture Sequences
Automated email and SMS sequences warm the lead with the right message at the right time — without anyone on your team lifting a finger.
04
Offer & Upsell
Order pages, upsell flows, and order bumps built to maximize average order value without adding friction to the purchase.
05
Ops Hand-off & Fulfillment
The sale auto-triggers your back-office: CRM updated, invoice generated, onboarding sequence started. The funnel and your ops speak the same language.
