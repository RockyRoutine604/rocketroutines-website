Common questions

Things people ask
before they book

What does the free audit actually involve?
A focused 30-minute Google Meet call. We map your current tools, identify your top three manual bottlenecks, and quantify what they're costing you in time and money. You'll receive a written output after the call — no pitch, no obligation.
When does the monthly retainer start?
Only after your first automation is live and working. The setup fee covers everything from discovery through to launch. You don't pay monthly until the system is delivering results.
How long does the setup phase take?
Typically 2–4 weeks for Type 1 retainer builds, depending on complexity. Type 2 custom builds vary based on scope — we'll give you a clear timeline during the paid scoping phase.
What if I'm not sure which tier I need?
That's exactly what the free audit figures out. We'll assess your current state, understand your goals, and recommend the right tier — or tell you if a custom build is a better fit.
Can I upgrade tiers as my needs grow?
Yes — and most clients do. We scope the initial engagement to where you are now, then expand as the system proves its value. There's no penalty for upgrading, and the transition is seamless.
Do you work with businesses outside Canada?
Yes. We serve clients across North America — Canada and the United States. All calls are via Google Meet and all work is delivered remotely.
What platforms do you integrate with?
We integrate with 20+ platforms including N8N, Zapier, Close.com, Airtable, Google Sheets, Float, BambooHR, Lattice, QuickBooks, NetSuite, SAP, JaneApp, Mariana Tek, and many more. If it has an API or documentation, we can connect it.
What makes Rocket Routines different from an automation freelancer?
We don't just build workflows — we design growth systems. That means understanding your entire operation, connecting all your platforms, and staying embedded as your ops partner. We think in outcomes, not outputs.
