How we work with you

The practical details that matter when you're evaluating whether Rocket Routines is the right fit.

Done-for-you, always
You never need to learn N8N, manage workflows, or debug broken integrations. We handle everything end-to-end. Your job is to tell us what's slowing you down — ours is to fix it.
Fixed pricing, no surprises
Setup fees are quoted upfront and fixed. Monthly retainers are flat — no hourly billing, no "it took longer than expected" overruns. What we quote is what you pay.
Retainer starts after results
We don't charge monthly until your first automation is live and working in production. The setup fee covers everything up to launch. The retainer only begins when the system is delivering value.
Minimal time required from you
The audit is 30 minutes. The kickoff call is 45 minutes. After that, monthly check-ins and a quarterly planning call. We operate autonomously — you're not our project manager.
Monthly reporting, always visible
You receive a written performance report every month — workflows active, runs completed, hours saved, issues caught and resolved. The ROI is always visible and documented.
The system grows with you
Most clients start with 2–3 automations and build 1–2 more each month. The system compounds over time — the more workflows are connected, the more value each new one delivers.
