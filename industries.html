Platform spotlight

The JaneApp & Mariana Tek
automation gap

Both platforms are excellent at what they do — booking, scheduling, and client management. But they leave significant automation gaps that cost gyms and wellness clinics hours every week. We close them.

JaneApp
JaneApp
The leading practice management platform for healthcare and wellness clinics — physio, chiro, massage, mental health, and more.
Automation gaps we close
  • 01 Lead capture → JaneApp: Enquiries from your website or ads don't auto-create a client record. We connect the gap so every lead is in the system instantly.
  • 02 Re-engagement triggers: No built-in automation for clients who stop booking. We trigger re-engagement sequences based on attendance gaps.
  • 03 Cross-platform reporting: JaneApp reporting stays in JaneApp. We pipe the data into dashboards that combine all your locations and metrics.
  • 04 Review request automation: Post-visit review requests aren't automated natively. We trigger them at the right moment to maximise conversion.
Mariana Tek
Mariana Tek
The enterprise fitness platform built for boutique studios — cycling, yoga, Pilates, HIIT, and multi-location gym groups scaling their operations.
Automation gaps we close
  • 01 Membership lapse sequences: Members who don't renew get no automated follow-up. We build renewal sequences triggered 30, 14, and 7 days before expiry.
  • 02 New member onboarding: First booking is captured — but there's no automated welcome journey. We build it: welcome, intro offer, milestone triggers.
  • 03 Multi-location data consolidation: Revenue and attendance data per location requires manual exports. We automate the pipeline into a single live dashboard.
  • 04 Lead source attribution: Mariana Tek doesn't close the loop on which ads or campaigns drove bookings. We connect ad data to booking data automatically.
